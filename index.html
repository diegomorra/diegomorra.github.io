Diego Morra _ Bio

Diego Morra, born in 1994 in Cernusco sul Naviglio, grew up in Milan.

His passion for digital communication leads him to enroll in a professional institute for advertising graphics at the age of 14.

He becomes passionate about the world of graphic design and photography.

In 2014 he enroll at the Accademia di Belle Arti di Brera to pursue his dream of wasting a year doing things he hates.

In 2015 he quit Brera and passed the Politecnico di Milano admission test, where he starts his studies in Communication Design obtaining first a Bachelor degree in 2018 and then a master's degree with full marks in 2020.

He start to developed a growing interest in interaction design, human-computer interaction, and historical documentaries about World War II during his university years.

Meanwhile, a lot of things happen: he works as a waiter to support his studies, he starts small collaborations with advertising agencies and small clients as a graphic designer, he starts traveling around Italy to breathe asbestos in abandoned places but he can only take some pictures , his car engine explodes on the highway, he becomes a teach assistant in photography and design courses, he sets up his own business and becomes a freelance graphic and UX/UI designer working with professionals, studios and companies in Milan.

Today Diego is sitting on his sofa, writing this bio about himself in third-person.
